Sports Psychologist

Madison Borough FC

Madison Borough FC is seeking a motivated and passionate Sports Psychologist to support the mental wellbeing and performance of players across the club.

The successful candidate will work with players and coaching staff to develop confidence, focus, resilience, and a positive performance mindset both on and off the pitch.

This is an exciting opportunity to be part of a forward-thinking football club committed to player development and wellbeing.

Location: Madison Borough Training Ground

Position Type: Part-time / Consultant

Salary: Competitive / Depending on experience

Job Description

Job Title: Sports Psychologist

Department: Performance & Player Support

Location: Madison Borough Training Ground

Role Purpose

To support the psychological wellbeing, confidence, resilience, and performance of players at Madison Borough FC.

Key Responsibilities
  • Provide one-to-one support sessions with players.
  • Deliver workshops on confidence, focus, and performance mindset.
  • Support players dealing with pressure, setbacks, or injuries.
  • Work alongside coaches to improve player mental resilience.
  • Support academy players with development and wellbeing.
  • Promote a positive, professional, and supportive team environment.

Person Specification

Essential Skills and Qualities
  • Knowledge of psychology or sports psychology.
  • Excellent communication and listening skills.
  • Ability to build trust with players and staff.
  • Interest in football and athlete development.
  • Ability to remain calm and supportive in high-pressure situations.
Desirable Skills and Qualities
  • Previous experience working with athletes or sports teams.
  • Understanding of mental performance techniques.
  • Ability to deliver workshops or group sessions.
